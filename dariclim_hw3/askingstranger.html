Choice 1: You ask a stranger for directions.

"Excuse me," you call out to a shadowed stranger standing across the street. "Could I ask where I am? I think I'm lost."

The stranger doesn't seem to hear you.

"I'm trying to get to 32nd street," you say while approaching closer to the stranger.

Suddenly, he turns around and replies,

"Maybe I can tell you the way, for a reward." You sigh.

"I don't have my wallet on me or anything," you say.

"Those shoes look nice," the stranger says while eyeing your shoes longingly.

"Are you crazy?" you ask. "I'm not gonna give you my shoes, how will I walk home?"

"Maybe just one shoe?"

"One shoe?" you scoff. "You really think I would wanna hop my way home in one shoe instead?"

The stranger loses interest and starts walking away.

"Good luck staying lost," the stranger says.

"Wait wait!" you say, and take a shoe off.

"Here you go, one shoe, you can have it." The stranger takes the shoe happily.

"Now will you tell me the way?"

The stranger looks from his gaze and replies, "Uh yeah I have no idea where we are, mate." He bolts away with your one shoe in a hurry. Now you're lost, AND you have only one shoe.
