Choice 2: You jump the turnstile in the nearest subway station

You look around the subway station. No cops anywhere. Just a few people standing waiting for the train, and a homeless man sitting on the ground.

Maybe it's ok this one time to jump the turnstile? You hop over.

The homeless man stands up, and takes off his costume. Underneath is a cop fully dressed in police uniform. He looks sternly over towards you. You freeze immediately, and gulp.

You just got caught.

Try again?
